1. Lo maan lia-> In the key of F# minor
   

   
Play the chords in F# minor, you can change the key and play it from anywhere you want, but F# minor sounds the most correct
Chords:
F# minor
G# dim
A major
B minor 
C# minor
D major
E major

Also experiment in C# natural minor
Both C# minor and F# minor are potentially good fits because C# minor is just beside in the circle of fifths and thus has many chords in common, but the key is F# minor

Good chords-> F#minor D C#7 A

There is a good tutorial with capo, first learn that, and then do the same chords without capo
Also step 2 will be to learn with the 7 and 9th extension chords like B7 and Fmadd9 and step 3 will be to experiment with your own chord combinations in F# minor key. Just keep adding shit into chords and keep switching around chord combinations, and see if it sounds good
